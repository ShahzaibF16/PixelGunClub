How To Purchase PGC

Here is the step by step guide to buy PGC.

  1. Download MetaMask or Trust Wallet Applications..
  2. Purchase Binance Tokens (BNB) and send them to your MetaMask or Trust Wallet. On the home page of PixelGunClub click on “Purchase Tokens” button. This will open a Dapp Exchange Called “PancakeSwap”..
  3. A popup will open, click the check box “I understand”, this will load PixelGunClub into PancakeSwap..
  4. Make sure your MetaMask Wallet or Trust Wallet Is synced with PancakeSwap..
  5. Set the amount of PGC you would like to purchase, and set slippage to 12%. Then press Swap!.
  6. Welcome to the community!.
